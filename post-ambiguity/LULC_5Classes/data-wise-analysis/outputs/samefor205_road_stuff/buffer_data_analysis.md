An assessment of the road network buffers reveals a strong concentration of urban development near transportation corridors. Within a 1 km cumulative buffer of major roads, the land is 22.07% built-up. When expanding the catchment area to a 2 km cumulative buffer, the overall built-up density drops slightly to 19.21%. This indicates that while the immediate roadside is highly saturated, a massive proportion of Bihar's total urban footprint is still completely contained within a 2 km walk of its major road networks
